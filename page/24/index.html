Meet the History of Chungju Blooming along the Namhan River - Chungju, Chungbuk, South Korea

Chungju Chungju is the hometown of Jungwon culture. Prehistoric remains remain around the Namhan River, and in the Three Kingdoms era, it was a fierce battlefield to occupy Jungwon. It is also the place where Jungwongyeong was made among the 5 blind spots since the Three Kingdoms period. In January, a large amount of bronze artifacts from the early Iron Age were excavated in Hoam-dong, Chungju. In the early Iron Age tomb 19 artifacts were excavated all at once, including the Sejong bronze sword. Let's meet Chungju's history from prehistoric times to Goryeo dynasty along the Namhan River, which is dotted with intense historical traces and various ruins. Chungju, Chungbuk, South Korea.
