Many things to see and do around Cape Homi
If you have been deeply impressed by the sunrise of Homigot Sunrise Square, it is time to look around. The first place to visit is the National Lighthouse Museum. Located at Homigot Sunrise Square, the National Lighthouse Museum is Korea's only lighthouse museum. The National Lighthouse Museum provides a variety of information about the lighthouse, including the history of the lighthouse, including Korea, as well as the types and principles of the lighthouse.
Homigot Lighthouse, the tallest in Korea
There are many interesting exhibits, such as a diorama, which reproduces the life of the lighthouse keeper called the lighthouse keeper, and the experience of making Morse code using a touch screen. It is also interesting to look at the history and history of Korea's lighthouse. Korea's first lighthouse is Palmido Lighthouse in Palmido, Incheon. The Homigot Lighthouse, which can be found at the National Lighthouse Museum, is the tallest lighthouse in Korea.
National Lighthouse Museum is Korea's only lighthouse museum
Don't miss the Millennium Memorial Hall, which embodies the round sun. The Millennium Memorial Hall is an exhibition center where visitors can meet Pohang's geographic characteristics, history, and future vision with various panels and dioramas, and about 2,000 rare marine life fossils, including the world's first photosynthetic organism, stromatolite and fossil fossils. There is a museum and the Korea Seokpo Pohang Museum where 300 rare heads are gathered together.
