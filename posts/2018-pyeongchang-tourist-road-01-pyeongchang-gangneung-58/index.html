Was Nami Island an island from the beginning? no. The construction of Cheongpyeong Dam in 1944 unintentionally made it an inland island that borders Chuncheon, Gangwon-do and Gapyeong, Gyeonggi-do. Purchased and planted by various individuals in 1965, it is now a huge forest with thousands of trees.
Nice forest path is the attraction of Nami Island
On Nami Island, he spends his time walking through forest trails, riding bicycles, taking photos at drama locations, and experiencing art. It is a representative traditional market located in Chuncheon downtown. Every corner of the market is decorated with works of art and murals, and there is an alley where the old scenery remains deep inside. Adjacent to the Myeongdong Chicken Ribs Alley, it is a popular tourist attraction.
