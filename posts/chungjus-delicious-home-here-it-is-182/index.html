Chungju, Chungbuk, Korea

Terms that describe good restaurants such as model restaurants, local restaurants, recommended restaurants, Daemulim restaurants, food restaurants, top restaurants, and TV broadcast restaurants vary by region. The expression I stumbled across the city of Chungju a while ago in Chungju was 'Good Rice House'. Chungcheongbuk-do has been implemented since 2013. In Chungju City, a movie restaurant, Cheonnyeonhyang, Manna Rice House, and Sato Bonga were selected.
Sanchaejeongsik Indulge in its pure taste, Suanbo 'Movie Restaurant'
Even before the Maika boom in Korea, this restaurant was rumored to be delicious. Even now, he continues his reputation and has also been on the list of “Best Houses with Good Rice” selected by Chungcheongbuk-do Province.
Suanbo Movie Restaurant Exterior
When I visited the movie restaurant opened in 1980 and wondered about the process of cooking, I readily agree. As soon as the three-person family ordered a set meal, they put a stone pot on the gas fire and put the soaked rice. Pour water over it and add 3 more beans. This is a common sight. When the rice water starts to boil, the aunt in charge of cooking the rice takes a small amount of the rice and adjusts the amount. It is said to be the only method of movie restaurant. Rice is purchased from Suanbo Agricultural Cooperatives. Soybeans are used in the summer in a mixture of three types: hedge beans, frost tae, juwolyangdae, and peas. It takes about 15 minutes for delicious rice to be completed. Immediately after cooking, the miso soup is served on another gas fire. When it boils, rice is finished.
3 beans in rice
During this process, while the rice is being cooked, the side dish chef puts 30 herbs into a small dish. Each dish is labeled with herbs. Medicinal constitutional rice is used to soothe Yeoksu, Yeonsu-dong 'Cheonnyanghyang'
'Food and drug mobilization (食 藥 同 原)'. Rice and medicine have the same roots, so if you meet good food on the road, you will feel tired. When you enter Cheonnyeonhyang, one of the ‘good-tasting homes’ in Chungju, the smell of medicinal herbs is soft. This restaurant is famous for its medicinal herb constitutional rice. It is run by Park Dong-soo, a holder of a health food prescription license. Above all, it is unique that customers can order and eat rice according to their constitution. Sitting in front of the sieve discriminator and recognizing the ten-finger print, it turns out that he is the owner of one of the constitutions: Taeinin, Taeumin, Soyangin, and Soumin.
Fingerprint recognition constitution discriminator at the entrance to Cheonnyeonhyang

Rice water also uses herbal decoction water suitable for constitution. It is also fun to read the instructions on the back of the sieve while food is served.

The restaurant's name is 'Manna Rice House', but many people are confused with 'Mana Rice House'. The people of Chungju said that if they drank excessively the previous day or had no appetite, they would use a bowl of Haejangguk to loosen the bitter stomach and sweat. Restaurant owner Eun-sook Kim has been running Haejanggukjib for 23 years near the Chungju Courthouse and has moved on to its reputation five years ago.
Manna Rice House Entrance
It's Haejanggukjip. In all cities, why was this restaurant selected as a “good rice house”? I'm curious about how to cook the rice after eating the ingredients of Haejangguk. Once rice is grown in the Chungbuk region, it is supplied from the Agricultural Cooperatives in Chungju. Add a little bit of millet to make the rice taste better. And use a pressure cooker with 12 servings. If you use a larger rice cooker, it will be difficult to maintain the proper taste. That is the secret of delicious rice.
Information on the “Good Rice House” at the Manna Rice House
Haejangguk is cooked with yellow persimmon oil in perilla oil, and then cooks the broth for about 20 hours. Add boiled sprouts, eggs, and yellow beans to it, and add perilla powder. The soup is white and the taste is savory. Gangwon-do Hwangtae Haejangguk and Jeonju Bean Sprout Haejangguk are different flavors. Perilla oil and perilla powder met to create the flavor of Haejangguk, a unique restaurant. Seasoning with shrimp sauce and dipping green pepper in a side dish of kkakdugi makes it easier for the stomach to feel uncomfortable with a few drinks last night. You can also try barley rice and mungdu samgyetang (reserved 2 hours before summer).

Bringing back the feeling of hometown with Cheonggukjang
From Sancheok-myeon Office, go to Jungwon Golf Course about 3km and you will find a restaurant called Sato Bonga. "The three things are important to making rice well," said Park Hyup-gyu, CEO of the restaurant, which opened in 2003. The first good rice, the second rice time, the third steaming time. It uses rice produced in the Chungbuk region and cooks 20 servings of rice in a caster. When steaming, put a pot on top of the rice cooker lid to get the pressure cooker effect. The steaming time is about 30 minutes. Seasonal rice is mixed with chajo, millet, and black rice.
The appearance of Sato Bonga where Cheonggukjang is delicious
This carefully cooked rice meets the savory Cheonggukjang, which has little odor, and becomes a whole meal. Cheonggukjang floats two or three times a week, and the amount is about two horses (14kg) at a time. Soybeans are also farmed and used by local residents. Since no imported beans are used, one bowl of Cheonggukjang is 8,000 won, which is 1,000 to 2,000 won more expensive than the other. Boil tofu, radish, zucchini, old paper, green onion, cheonyang pepper, and onion in cheonggukjang. Neither anchovies nor pork. The taste of Cheonggukjang is still alive.
Sato Bonga's Cheonggukjang
As a dessert, Nurungji Sung-guk is served. I don't need any other dessert because I eat the salty paper that I made last fall as a side dish.
