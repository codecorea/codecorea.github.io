Geumsan-gun, Chungnam, South Korea

Geumsan, the home of ginseng, is a mountainous area located near Buyeo, the capital of Baekje. Many people gather at Geumsan where the national ginseng festival is held every fall during the harvest season. We stepped ahead and headed to Geumsan one summer day before the first blessing. Traveling along the ginseng body, go go!
Geumsan Ginseng has a history of more than 1,500 years.
Once upon a time, more than a thousand years ago. In Geumsan, there lived Gang Sae's house in Seonggokri. Kang Hyo-ja, a well-known Hyoja, runs back to life to save her mother when her mother is sick. Then, I prayed and prayed for healing my mother's disease at Jinaksan Gwaneum cave. As the spirit of the mountain was rooted, my mother was completely relieved.
Geumsan Ginseng Center called “heart” of Geumsan Ginseng Market
Here is not the end. Gangchusa handed out the seeds of the perennial plant to the local people, who carefully planted the seeds and healed them with thick roots. The root of the grass resembles a human being more mysterious. That's why he got the name 'Ginseng (人蔘)'.
Fresh ginseng weighing 42,000 won for 750g
This is the legend of Geumsan Ginseng. Geumsan began to plant grass that grew in the mountains. 946, Seonggok-ri, Namnam, Geumsan-gun, where Gangchusa first planted ginseng is called 'gasamsam'. This is where ginseng began. Every year, thanks to Sanshin and Gangchusa who helped grow ginseng, they celebrate the good harvest.
Ginseng Market in Geumsan-eup
The history of Geumsan Ginseng can also be found in the records. In Kim Bu-sik's Samguk Sagi, there is a record that Baekje Muryeong King sent “Baekjesam” as a gift to the Chinese Yang dynasty. Geumsan is a mountainous area near Baekje capital, Buyeo, and it can be inferred that Baekjesam's main production site was Geumsan. In the Joseon Dynasty, Sinjeung-dong Guyeojiseungram, Jinsan-myeon, Geumsan-gun, records Ginseng. After that, after the Japanese colonial period and the Korean War, Geumsan became the home of ginseng that is responsible for the production and sale of ginseng.
Geumsan International Ginseng Market
Can a festival fall out of ginseng? Around September, the annual harvest season of ginseng, Geumsan is thrilled with medicinal merchants and tourists from all over the country. At the same time, the Geumsan Ginseng Festival will be held. Participants go directly to the ginseng field to harvest ginseng, select good ginseng, sample ginseng dishes, and enjoy various ginseng experiences. Buying ginseng at an affordable price is a bonus for those who visit the festival. Of course, even if not during the harvest season and festival season, you can always get high quality ginseng in Geumsan. Ginseng that boosts immunity, where to live.

If you step outside the door, it is the bottom of the Geumsan ginseng market, which accounts for 80% of ginseng production and distribution. Geumsan International Ginseng Market, Geumsan Fresh Ginseng Center and Geumsan Fisheries Wholesale Market are located.
Mr. Ginseng King
To buy fresh ginseng, take a look around the Geumsan Fresh Ginseng Center, where fresh ginseng from all over the country gathers and holds auctions every 1 to 6 days. You can find a variety of fresh ginseng at affordable prices. Fresh ginseng means non-dried biological ginseng. White ginseng is dried without processing, and red ginseng is cooked and boiled. In addition, depending on the quality of the product and the time of cultivation, it is also divided into ginseng, rice, and ginseng.

Fortunately, there is a ginseng porridge village in Geumsan. Ginseng specialty shops are gathered in Cheonna-ri, Jeogok-ri, and Yonghwa-ri, which wear Geumgang. The thick and thick ginseng is deadly. Spicy yet juicy, light and thick. Ginseng's bitter scent is added to ordinary fish porridge. Nearby restaurants also sell ginseng porridge, torii bang bang and freshwater fish, as well as ginseng tempura. Either way, add some appetite.
Ginseng Samgyetang, the best summer food
Ginseng Samgyetang can be tasted in the town where Geumsan Ginseng Market is located.
