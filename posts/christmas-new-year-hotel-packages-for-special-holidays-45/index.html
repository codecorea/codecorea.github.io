Christmas & New Year Hotel Packages “For Special Holidays”

Jung-gu, Seoul, Korea

The cold winds make us lighter in our pockets. Here is a little extravagant hotel package for you who have been running hard for a year. Let's take a look just in case. Who knows and will meet someone who wants to make use of the Christmas hotel package!

Christmas package presented at InterContinental Seoul <Picture Credit: InterContinental Seoul>
Grand Ambassador Seoul

The All About Eve package is ready for romantic Christmas Eve. Christmas dinner at the banquet hall on the 19th floor with a panoramic night view of Namsan Mountain is included. Starting at 429000 won. There is also a “sweet holiday package” for romantic time only. It consists of one night, a bottle of wine and sweet macaroons in a cozy superior room for 24 and 31 days. Starting at 25,9600 won. There is also a “Namsan Sunrise Package” where you can prepare for the New Year while watching the sunrise at Namsan, the symbol of Seoul. Coffee and hot packs are useful for trekking in Namsan, and two room service breakfasts and a sauna pass for relaxing after sunrise. Only one day on December 31st. Starting at 28,3800 won. All guests have free access to the hotel's swimming pool and fitness. VAT 10% not included. Inquiries and reservations 02-2270-3111.

https://grand.ambatel.com/seoul/main.amb

Christmas Package 'All About Eve' <Photo courtesy of Grand Ambassador>
Grand Hilton Seoul

Grand Hilton Seoul has prepared three “Love At Christmas” packages only for a week from 19th to 25th for those who dream of a sweet and romantic Christmas. 'Love et Christmas 1' includes a Deluxe Room, Happy Hour Promo, where you can enjoy a bottle of red wine and unlimited draft beer from 6 pm to 9 pm. 'Love Et Christmas 2' will have dinner tickets added to the upgraded room. If you're dreaming of a day in a junior suite, you can use Love At Christmas 3. The price starts from 220,000 won. Contact 02-2287-8400.
http://www.grandhiltonseoul.com/

▶ Lotte Hotel Seoul

Lotte Hotel Sogong Branch will be hosting the 2014 Christmas Warmth Package from December 24 to 26. Superior rooms, breakfast buffet, Lotte Hotel bear doll, Christmas cake and wine, and mini Christmas tree. The Sweet Package, which includes upgraded rooms for juniors, will have room service for two Christmas special dinners. From 390,000 won.

Christmas Package <Photo courtesy of Lotte Hotel Seoul>

There is also a Happy New Year 2015 package on the last day of the year. There are `` Basic Packages '' which include superior rooms, breakfast, New Year's cakes, Zolme dolls to celebrate the Year, and `` Suit Packages '' to enjoy meals in upgraded rooms. From 320,000 won. Tax and service charge separately. Contact 02-759-7311
http://www.lottehotelseoul.com/kr/

Happy New Year 2015 Package (Photo courtesy of Lotte Hotel Seoul)
Mayfield Hotel

At Mayfield Hotel, the Christmas & Year-end package My Sweet Heart is available in three versions from December 24 to 31. It includes a "sweet box" containing imported sweets, jelly and candy, and a "sweet heart delivery" mailing of love letters.
"My Sweet Heart" version 1 features a Superior Room and a Michelin 2-Breakfast Buffet, while version 2 offers Christmas headbands and Santa Helena wine for Christmas Eve customers only. Version 3 has Christmas Eve performances and dinners. Starting at 276,000 won. Tax and service charge separately. Contact 02-2660-9000.

http://www.mayfield.co.kr/

Grand InterContinental Seoul

Grand InterContinental Seoul has a "Christmas of Love" package that adds cinema to your hotel break. Packages for the modern people who want to enjoy both relaxing and cultural life in the city include a chocolate basket and a bottle of wine. It also offers tickets to the Winter Festival Christmas Market booth held in the COEX complex. There is also a 'Celebrating 2015 Package' for the new year. In 2015, you can enjoy unlimited wine at the lobby lounge of the hotel, based on healing in the city, and experience Bongeunsa and other fireworks. Christmas packages start at 305,000 won and New Year packages start at 295,000 won. Tax and service charge separately. Contact 02-3430-8888
https://www.grandicparnas.com:444/kor/index.do

Celebrating 2015 Package (Photo courtesy of InterContinental)
Imperial Palace Seoul

Imperial Palace Seoul has prepared an “In the mood for love” package for a romantic Christmas in the heart of the city. Includes room, breakfast, real wood candle & saucer set. If you choose a room as a suite, room service such as wine, pizza, chocolate, and mini cakes is added. There is also an “In the Mood for Love-Special Eve” package where you can enjoy Christmas parties in your room. This is a special package that includes two tickets to the Lobby Lounge Del Mar's Christmas party in the “In the mood for love” package. There is also a New Year's Eve package that will allow you to enjoy the year-end party and the New Year. Christmas packages start at 290,000 won and New Year packages start at 220,000 won. Tax and service charge separately. Contact 02-3440-8000.
http://www.imperialpalace.co.kr/
