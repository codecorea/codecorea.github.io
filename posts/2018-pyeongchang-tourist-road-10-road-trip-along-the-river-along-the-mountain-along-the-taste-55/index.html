The road trip in Gangwon-do is lyrical. When viewed from a distance, it has a river that seems to stop and sparkle and a museum that resembles a calm river. The place where people gather along the river naturally stands and is full of food. That's why my trip is full of excitement. Let's go on a road trip to Gangwon-do, where you can travel like a river and fill your heart and enjoy the taste.
Road trip begins at Nami Island floating on the Bukhan River. Travelers are famous for the drama 'Winter Sonata' and become accustomed to it. The attraction of Nami Island is that it makes you feel as if you have traveled abroad in Korea. So I don't know if it's called Naminara. There are two ways to enter Nami Island. 5 minutes by boat, or a straw wire, 30-40 km per hour to fly in 1 minute 30 seconds.
The most popular metasequoia road on Nami Island
Stepping into the Republic of Naminara, you will see a forest of trees stretching high into the sky. If you walk along the cool shade, you will find a picture book playground, a restaurant, Metasequoia Road, and a hotel Jeonggwanru. You can walk as slowly as you can, or rent a bicycle or electric car to explore every corner. Cycling rides along the Metasequoia Road, Fir Road, Ginkgo Road and Birch Road in the wind, and the summer heat is forgotten.
Bicycles are an important means of transportation on Nami Island
The place where many tourists are attracted is the location of Winter Sonata. The metasequoia road, which looks like a scene from a foreign movie, and the place where Bae Yong-jun and Choi Ji-woo make and kiss a snowman are essential courses for lovers who dream of a romantic trip.
If you are with your children, I recommend you to stop by the picture book playground where picture books are placed on a large bookshelf that extends up to the ceiling of the second floor. There are more than 20,000 children's books on the first floor, and there are more than 5,000 picture books from 86 countries on the second floor.
Drama Winter Sonata is the theme of Nami Island's eternal love
Also remember that Nami Island has a tomb of General Nami. Nami Island was originally a land belonging to Chuncheon City, and it became an island when the Cheongpyeong Dam was built in 1944. Nami spent his life in exile in Nami Island, reversing the conspiracy as a mother of Yu Ja-gwang. Today's tomb is a mystery, and the actual tomb is in Hwaseong, Gyeonggi-do.
Cool motorboat running across the water
If you know that Nami Island is in Chuncheon, you should put chicken ribs in your itinerary. "If you come to Chuncheon do not eat chicken ribs does not mean come to Chuncheon," remember the saying of the travelers. There are many restaurants that display 'Chuncheon Chicken Ribs' sign everywhere in the big city, but it's not comparable to eating in Chuncheon. You will have to try Chuncheon Chicken Ribs before moving on to other destinations. Chuncheon chicken ribs are two kinds. There are chicken ribs grilled over charcoal and grilled chicken ribs with plenty of vegetables on the iron plate. The beginning of Chuncheon Chicken Ribs was to grill on charcoal, but with the spread of thick teppanyaki, the teppanyaki style became popular.
Chicken Ribs Cooked on Sizzling Grill
Chicken rib shops on the Nami Island marina are grilled with chicken, vegetables, and rice cakes on an iron plate. Its spicy and sweet taste makes it popular with foreign tourists. If you want to eat roasted chicken ribs over charcoal, you should go to Sinbuk-eup. Any type of chicken ribs wrapped in lettuce with plenty of meat and vegetables, and served with Makguksu will complete Chuncheon's delicacy.
Chicken ribs and makguksu are a combination of fantasy
The road bends and heads to Mt. Wonju Museum. Cross the hill to the sky and pass through the birch forest path to see the picturesque Museum Mt. The scene looks like a fairy tale scene or a private garden of a rich man in Europe. With the design of world-class architect Tadao Ando, light, water, stone, nature and architecture blend together as a work of art.
Each line of the building looks like a work of art
The water garden filled with calm water has a Seosan Haemistone on the floor that turns black when it touches the water. The calm water contains the sky, so the steps leading to the entrance of the exhibition hall seem to be walking in the air. Walk slowly and admire the formation of the lines and sculptures of the building reflected in the water depending on the angle of light. There is a work by James Turrell who works on the theme of light. It is an exhibition where you can experience a new perspective on light and space.
The water garden of Mt.Museum containing the sky
When talking about Pyeongchang, Lee Hyo-seok's novel `` When Buckwheat Peel '' is indispensable. The buckwheat blossoms, which wander around the country in fiction, are so beautiful that the buckwheat blossoms bloomed in beauty.
If you want to see the buckwheat's flesh, go to Bongpyeongjang.
There is a Bongpyeongjang that is the background of the novel. It is a permanent market, but on the day of the month 2 and 7, the oil market opens and the market is noisy. It is true that Bongpyeongjang, which has a 400-year history, has been in decline since the times changed. However, the local people joined forces to preserve the tradition and renovated. Tents and aprons were made by colorizing five items, agricultural products, seafood, food, clothing, and sundries. The merchants also made displays and made plates that marked the country of origin for the customers to see. Proud to show off signs and business cards with a merchant's face. On the day of the oil stand, there are about 100 shops, and there are many buckwheat restaurants near Bongpyeongjang.
Bongpyeongjang filled with buckwheat food and the smell of crowded people
Even on rainy days, you can have fun moving between tents and tents while listening to the sound of rain falling under the hood. Yega-Chef's hand-drip coffee, or a bowl of rice wine with buckwheat pancakes, can be tasted only at Bongpyeongjang.
Bongpyeong oil stand stands on the day of the 2nd and 7th every month
You can't miss buckwheat food from Bongpyeongjang. Buckwheat is grown throughout Gangwon-do, but buckwheat has become a modifier of Bongpyeong. Like the buckwheat, Bongpyeong has various buckwheat foods such as soba noodles, buckwheat pancake, buckwheat jelly, buckwheat pancake and soba liquor.
Buckwheat is famous for its variety.
On the day of the oil stand, there are many shops selling buckwheat pans and sorghum buckwheat inside the market. Kimchi, vermicelli, pork, vegetables, etc. are put in thinly buckwheat buckwheat before making it.
Cold buckwheat blows off summer heat
Soba noodles served with spicy fresh kimchi with garnish and sprinkled with sesame salt with plenty of vegetables, are popular with both young grandchildren and elderly grandmothers.
Rice cake and hand drip coffee
Mui-Art Gallery is 10 minutes away from Bongpyeongjang. Four artists, sculptor, potter, calligrapher, renovated Muyi Elementary School, which was closed. On the playground, where students played, there are green grass and about 100 sculptures by artist Oh Sang Wook. The interior of the school is filled with sculptures and exhibits, just like students arranged works in the hallway after the art class.
Muyi Art Museum
The sound of the squeaky wooden floor and the squeaky wood windows is soed every step of the way. The classroom was an exhibition hall where the artist's work was exhibited, and an open studio of the artist in full swing, so that you can meet the present and future of Bongpyeong art. If you are a young child with a mobile phone ring, fan making, print experience (group booking), you can leave new memories and souvenirs.
Art that fills the hallway
Go up to the second floor and there is a terrace where you can relax. It is a structure that protrudes slightly at one end of the school and has a great view overlooking the wide lawn and statues.
Elementary school classroom transformed into artist's studio and exhibition hall

-Chuncheon Romantic Market
It is a representative traditional market located in Chuncheon downtown. Every corner of the market is decorated with works of art and murals, and there is an alley where the old scenery remains deep inside. Adjacent to the Myeongdong Chicken Ribs Alley, it is a popular tourist attraction.
-Address: 34 Myeongdong-gil, Chuncheon-si, Gangwon-do
-Hours: From 8 o'clock (varies by store)
