The name of 'Nami Island' is due to Nami General (1441 ~ 1468). At the age of seventeen, there was a stone grave here known as the tomb of General Nami, who died for sixty six years under the mothership of Yoo Ja-gwang. During the development of Nami Island, the tombs were trimmed to make the Nami General Gamyo.

Nami Island Entrance
There are two ways to enter Nami Island: sky and water. You can take a boat (10,000 won) from Gapyeong Naru or fly on a straw wire (38,000 won).
Nami Island entrance scenery
If you select straw wire, you have to use the sky path only when entering Nami Island from Gapyeong and take a boat when you exit. The return wire is included in the straw wire fare. You can enter Nami Island in 3 ~ 5 minutes on the waterway and 1 minute and 30 seconds on the skyway. From March to the 1st and 3rd Mondays, the straw wire cannot be used for regular inspections.
