Maisan Cherry Blossom Road is filled with spring mood

'Cherry Blossom Ending' is on the way to Topsa, and Maisan Cherry Blossom Road

Jinan-gun, Jeollabuk-do, Korea

I missed the peak of spring flowers, is it just swallowing the sadness? Jinan Mai Mountain is located on a plateau, where cherry blossoms bloom until late. It is a bulwark of cherry blossom viewing. The shading of Maisan reflected in the Top Youngje Reservoir and the rare stone tower of Topsa serve as a “travel insurance” even after flowering. If you take a walk at Jinan Red Ginseng Spa or Baekwoon Village Village, it is a springtime destination regardless of flowers.
When the flowers bloom in Yunjung-ro, Maisan blooms
Spring flowers are in full bloom in April. It is a good time to go to the mountains. There are many cherry blossom paths. It blooms brightly and abundantly with springtime
The Maisan Cherry Blossom Road is about 2.5km from the tomb of Isan to Topsa. Maisan cherry trees are 20-30 years old and are not large or thick. However, the road toward Sansa adds a sense of luck. It shines in harmony with the surrounding nature. The slope to the top temple is gentle, so walk slowly and cherry blossoms
Maisan Cherry Blossom Road is perfect for walking
Top Youngje, decal comani of spring day
From the road leading to the southern parking lot in Maisan, the streets of cherry blossoms make you feel the peak of spring. When you get off the parking lot, move towards the tower. A quiet cherry blossom path opens.
Restaurants are located at the entrance of Maisan Cherry Blossom Road
The first part is a restaurant around the ticket office. There are many foods in Jinan such as fried ginseng or black pork. It is a place to stop by and enjoy entertainment. It is not easy to overlook if you are hungry.
Representative menu of the cherry blossom road entrance Grilled black pork of Jinan
The nature of cherry blossoms in nature grows as they pass through the Golden Temple. It's the last cherry blossom, so there aren't many late spring visitors, but somewhere in the spring. Nevertheless, the pink cherry blossom branches hanging along the roadside are a constant excitement.
Cherry blossoms blooming in front of the Golden Temple
The essence is Top Youngje Reservoir. It makes Maisan cherry blossoms famous regardless of time. Cherry blossoms along the side of the reservoir
Cherry Blossom Road Bench at Top Youngje Reservoir
If it is a late spring flower outing, the reflection of the young Maisan in the Topyeongje Reservoir will soothe the sadness. Ammaibong and the green of the cherry tree that just sprouted cast a soft shadow on the water.
Reflection of young cherry blossom path and Ammaibong of Topyeongje Reservoir
Decalcomani of Mother Nature is a painting and art. It is a view with elasticity. If you sit still on the bank, it is full of spring energy.
The reflection of Maisan on the waterside is a spring day.
Classic boating is also tempting. It is a duck boat going up and down the water. If you are a lover, you are encouraged to enjoy the scenery of spring on the water.
Duck riding adds classic romance
If you hang over the edge of the cherry blossoms, the petals will fly like snowflake on the water, giving you a romantic moment.
Cherry blossoms blend in the reservoir landscape at sunset
Looking for a springtime spa or “signboard”
The stone tower experience area passes as well as a trip to Topyeongje and goes to Topsa Temple. You can stop walking and build a stone tower. If you are with a family member or a lover, you may wish to add a light wish.
Green and pink cherry blossoms harmonize and decorate spring
Cherry blossoms hang over the rustic stone tower. And when the last cherry blossoms disappear, Pagoda Temple is revealed. Top Temple also acts as an 'insurance' for late flower viewing. Maisan is a must-visit destination with a panoramic view of the tower.
Pagoda's towers are mysterious at any time, regardless of season.
As the name suggests, Pagoda is surrounded by huge towers. These are the 80 stone towers built by Gap-ryong Lee. Among them, Cheonji Tower, Moonlight Tower, and Yaksa Tower are famous. In this, it contained the rationale of the microcosm of Yin and Yang. Most of all, it is mysterious that it has not collapsed in the typhoon for over 100 years. Walk in between and climb to the top pair of heaven and earth. From the back of Cheonji Pagoda
Stacking stones at the Stone Tower Experience Center is also a small pleasure.
After visiting Pagoda Temple, you can pass through Eunsu Temple and Emperor Gate to descend to the northern parking lot. It is also recommended to enjoy the mountain hike in person. Starting from the southern parking lot, climb to Gogeumdang, Observatory, and Bongdubong, then descend to Topsa, or from the observation deck to the northern parking lot, the course takes 2 to 2 hours and 30 minutes. It is a road full of fresh green in springtime.
Cheonji Tower from the top of Pagoda Temple and surrounding mountains
After visiting the cherry blossoms of Maisan or going on a mountain hike in spring, you can make a trip to Jinan Red Ginseng Spa. Jinan is famous for ginseng, but red ginseng spa is a famous place to experience its efficacy.
Open-air hot spring at Jinan Red Ginseng Spa
Jinan Red Ginseng Spa is largely divided into Taiji Zone, Therapy Zone, and Aqua Zone. The Taegeuk Zone, which is a massage method with red ginseng foam, is the special treatment for the Taegeuk Bubble Sense Therapy and the Aqua Zone open-air bath while enjoying the view of Maisan Mountain. You can also spend a day in Jinanville, a lodging facility, and travel to Jinan.
Representative spa facility Taegeuk Bubble Sense Therapy <Photo courtesy, Jinan Red Ginseng Spa>
