Hapcheon-gun, Gyeongnam, Korea

Hapcheon has a quiet but beautiful scenery. Headed by the Haeinsa Temple at the foot of Gayasan, he prepared a healing trip to soothe the mind and body, focusing on Odosan Sunrise, Hapcheon Film Theme Park and Samga Hanwoo Street.
This line starts at Gayasan and Haeinsa, which are located in the northernmost part of Hapcheon, and meets one by one as it goes down to the south.

What kind of locality is it? Hapcheon means “narrow mine”. If you read Chinese characters literally, it is Hyecheon. There are many mountains and no fields. The narrow valley surrounded by mountains is named after many topographical features. However, in March 1914, when the administrative district was reorganized, it was said that the sum of the three villages was combined as the basins of Chogye and Samga were incorporated into Hapcheon-gun.
