2016 selected tourist destination “Boryeong Daecheon Beach”

Boryeong, Chungnam, Korea

Daecheon Beach is the largest beach on the west coast (3.5km long white sand beach, 100m wide) where you can enjoy a beautiful sunset with gentle slopes and clean sand that has been a recreational complex for foreigners since the 1930s. In particular, this beach is famous as a summer tourist destination attracting the attention of the world and attracting attention from the world and abroad as a mud festival held every year aimed at summer.

The laughter of people who covered their bodies with mud never stops. This is the story of the Mud Festival held in the Boryeong Daecheon Beach. There are many things to enjoy besides mud massage. From sky bikes, sea baths to soothe the heat of the day, to mud baths and bubble baths. I don't think there is such a place to enjoy the hot summer. Above all, all of these are open to anyone. It is a record of travel with members of the Chungnam Severity Independent Living Association.
Aka 'open carpet'
Sea open to all
The area around Daecheon Beach became more beautiful. It is the realization of the value of enjoying the beach and the sea together without discrimination. Named 'Open Sea'.
Sea access using an underwater wheelchair
Twenty 10-meter carpets were laid on the sandy beach, and the US patented 'water chair' was prepared. Instead of a sandy beach that falls completely, even people with physical disabilities who cannot move themselves by riding a water chair rolling a carpet can enter the beach.
Sea bathing experience with safety personnel
Experience underwater swimming with the help of safety personnel on an underwater wheelchair
In addition, the scenery where three sturdy staff members hold water chairs and help people with disabilities to bathe is impressive. The sea, which was felt far and wide by people with physical disabilities, was wide open. The faces of people with disabilities who have experienced things they couldn't even do are full of laughter.
Prepare a safe space for people with disabilities to experience mud
The impression of the moment when the cool sea water touches the body is revealed with a facial expression, and the expressions of people looking at the scenery also shine brightly as if they realized the 'value of the same'. The sea was opened for the handicapped, and it is the moment when the mind is opened to the handicapped.

The impression is doubled in the kids zone, a little away from the main venue. Although the name is Kids Zone, it is an open experience area that can be used by the elderly and the disabled. The kids zone, which has been prepared for safety since this year, can be reached in a minute by tram car from the second watchtower. There are a variety of things to do, such as bubble experience, mud experience, and water slide. Facilities are important, but opening your mind is a priority. That way, everyone can be happy together. The meaning of 'together' is deeply carved into the heart, and the heart is united. The place of the Boryeong Mud Festival that is enjoyable without anyone being alienated is so beautiful.
Unfortunately, the 2017 Boryeong Mud Festival ended on July 31st. However, Sky Bike is available all year round, and the open sea is available during the opening of the beach.
Sky bike in harmony with the sea landscape
Stairs, not obstacles, with human service at all times-Sky Bike
If you don't want to immerse yourself in the sea water and experience mud, try sky bike.
Stairs not obstacles due to human service operation
This is a 4-person bike ride along a rail installed on a sandy beach and runs 2.3 km over the sea.
Safe boarding with staff assistance
Unfortunately, there is no wheelchair ramp from the ticket office to the platform.
Skybike boarding completed
However, there are no inconveniences because the staff are always on standby to help the disabled with wheelchair use, and human services are provided. It is also the staff's responsibility to help the wheelchair sit in the bike.
Skybike for the disabled
There is no difficulty in accessing the stairs of the doorway by operating the staff to guide you on boarding the Sky Bike.
The bike with the guardian begins to move forward little by little. From the crowded beaches to the horizon far away from the crowded people, you can see the beautiful scenery of the ocean in front of Daecheon at a glance. Although it is sunny, the wind blows at the speed of running. If it's still hot, you can unfold the rainbow-colored umbrellas, each plugged into the back of the front seat. It would be fun if it were flat. There are 203 meters and 173 meters downhill, respectively, on the way to the loop point and on the way back from the loop point to the platform. The maximum speed of the downhill section is 20 km / h. Because of the high tide time, the thrill of enjoying on the sea filled with water is dizzy. Of course, if there is a downhill, there is also an uphill. Fortunately, the uphill course is equipped with an automatic driving system on the rail, so you don't have to pedal hard.
