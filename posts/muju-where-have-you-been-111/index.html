Fireflies light up tail lights only in clean areas. Muju is the place that has the most signals to indicate that it is clear water, clean air, and unpolluted land. Why is it that Bandiland is located in Muju?
Bondiland is a place where you can see various insects in your eyes. This is a customized, hands-on learning space for children. In addition to the largest insect museum in Korea, where you can meet rare insects in a large space of more than 120,000㎡, there are various facilities such as youth camping ground, natural recreation forest facility, firefly natural school, and astronomical science museum.

Meet the soul of Taekwondo, Taekwondowon
Taekwondo, representing Korea. The sacred place of Taekwondo is right in Muju. Muju Taekwondowon, the only specialty Taekwondo space in Korea and abroad, is half the size of Yeouido. It is a dream place for the world's taekwondo people who have everything about taekwondo, including not only museums but also international games, experiences, training, education, and research.
Taekwondo Museum where you can learn about Taekwondo
Everything from the spirit of Taekwondo to the fields of technology and the history of the development of Taekwondo can be seen at the National Taekwondo Museum. From the military martial arts of ancient Korean nationalism to the folk martial arts of the Joseon Dynasty, and to the present, the history of Taekwondo is melted in a three-dimensional exhibition space. In addition, Yap! Is a hands-on experience hall where you can experience the T1 performance hall where you can see your skills and skills. There is a `` challenge for challenge '', so visitors can go one step closer to Taekwondo.
