While looking for a sledding range to enjoy the day, I decided to visit the Ttukseom River Park sledding range and the Children's Hall sledding range that are suitable for 'convenient transportation' and 'low price'. I added the Seoul Plaza Skating Rink which adds a more romantic atmosphere (because winter nights in the city are beautiful). Let's go to Ttukseom Hangang Park sledding range first.

Transformation Ttukseom Han River Park Pool! Sledding Range in Winter!
Ttukseom Hangang Park Sledding Range is located in Ttukseom Hangang Park. You will see it immediately after exit 2 of Ttukseom Park Station. Based on the sledding grounds, it has a small play area such as a mini viking, an air bumper car and a room. There is also a winter celebrity, smelt fishing experience (4,000 won), which is perfect for a half-day playground. Yeouido sledding range which opened together did not open this year.
Low slopes are available for children
Ttukseom Hangang Park Sledding Range, which opened on December 18, is scheduled to operate until February 22. The original notice went out to February 15, but said it would extend the operating period to 22 days at the on-site ticket booth. Hours of operation are from 9:00 am to 5:00 pm on weekdays and weekends. There is no day off but may be temporarily adjusted in case of bad weather. The sledding playground will be closed for one hour every day from noon. Remember
Low slopes are available for children
Tickets for sledding ranges at 6,000 won, and free passes including the trampoline, bomber car, minibikes and electric cars are 10,000 won. Admission ticket for smelt fishing experience in the sledding range is 4000 won.
More fun with mom and dad!
Let's start with the sledding range. As it can be used for more than 36 months, it is equipped with a 30m small sledding range and an 80m large sledding range to prevent safety accidents. The small size is only available for under 6 years from 36 months. In addition, plastic sleds and tube sleds are available for various sled experiences.
More fun with mom and dad!
Adults like it more! Sledding Range next to Children's Grand Park!
Many people visited the smelt fishing experience area next to the sledding range. Only 10 can be caught at a time, but even those who haven't visited the smelt festival can enjoy catching smelt. The smelt from the smelt fishing experience field is taken to a shelter next to it and washed for 2000 won and then fried. Fish cakes and drinks are available. If you want to have a cup of tea while looking at the Han River after exciting sleds and smelt fishing, head to the Ttukseom Viewing Culture Complex. The sledding tour can be completed at a cultural retreat with a library and café.
Squid fishing without words!
The Children's Hall sledding range is not far from Ttukseom River Park sledding range. Located next to Children's Grand Park, it is also called Children's Grand Park Sledding Range. It also operates until February 22. Exit at Children's Grand Park Station (Line 7) and go straight 300m.
Squid fishing without words!
Children's Hall sledding range is 10,000 won and set ticket with smelt fishing experience is 15,000 won. Additional charges for Air Bumbercar (KRW 5,000), Euro address (KRW 5,000), and Viking (KRW 3000) are additional. It is open daily from 9:30 am to 5:00 pm, without opening. You must enter by no later than 4:00.
Freshly caught smelt can be fried immediately
There is also a gentle 80m course slope dedicated to children to prevent accidents. Mom and dad can use it with their children. There is also a sledding range for adults up to 120 meters in length, which is a great date course.
Freshly caught smelt can be fried immediately
Downtown night view is a bonus, Seoul Plaza Skating Rink
Seoul Square Skating Rink In winter, the plaza in front of the Seoul City Hall is reborn as an ice rink. It will be open until February 8, which will open on December 19. Hours are 10am to 9:30 pm (Sun-Thu) / 10am to 11pm (Saturday and Public Holidays).
The money needed to skate while enjoying the night view in the middle of downtown Seoul is 1,000 won. The price includes the skate rental fee. It will be open for 1 hour and then 30 minutes for maintenance from 10 am. 700 people each day. Internet and field booking are half and half.
