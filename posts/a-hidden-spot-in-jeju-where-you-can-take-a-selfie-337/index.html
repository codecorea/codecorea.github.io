Seongsidol Ranch, full of exotic emotions
A unique architecture standing alone at the edge of a blue field. Seongsidol Ranch is one of the places not to be missed when it comes to wedding photography on Jeju Island. The idyllic and peaceful atmosphere is a popular destination among photographers and photographers. Even though it is located in a remote place in the western mountains, the steps to find the knowing knowing constantly.
Take the Jungmun Expressway, pass Saebyeol Oreum, and follow the side road on the right. The road to the ranch is very lyrical. Horses grazing or resting among tall trees lined up on either side of the road unfold like a wonderful piece. After that, the field with soft grass is infinitely relaxed.
Horses grazing in the field by the way to the ranch
Around the edge of the ranch, there is a landmark building, the Teshphon. It is the most famous shooting spot in the Seongsidol Ranch. In many places, many commemorative photos, artworks and selfies are born. Tessiphone is a unique structure built in Baghdad, Iraq, about 2,000 years ago. It was said that it was used as a lodging facility when establishing a ranch, and has since been used for various purposes, such as a pig farm or feed mill. It is now a great shooting accessory for prospective brides and grooms.
Tessiphone, the landmark of Seongsidol Ranch
Photo enthusiasts and ordinary travelers also visit Tessiphone a lot. It looks old and shabby, but in the picture it looks very cool and exotic. Everyone becomes the main character of the pictorial here. On good days, you can get great pictures no matter how you take them. However, when it is not, it may be more disappointing than expected, so when the day is not good, it is better to empty your mind and go.
Teshphone, a photo spot
Teshphone is also the filming location for the 2014 drama Drama Mama. This is where Song Yoon-a went to teach her son a motorcycle. After filming and post-processing on the dash phone, it was aired in Canada.
