Flower Palace with Plum, Camellia, and Wildflowers

If you've been obsessed with these worries once, how about going to Haenam? There is no Bohae plum plantation on the mountain side of Haenam, so it is rather a relaxing flower garden. 14,000 plum trees are planted in a field of 46,000 square meters, which is the largest in Korea. White plum blossoms are the main species, but intermittent red plum blossoms are also mixed. Camellia acts as a fence on both sides of the flower garden. Under the plum blossoms, it is a green meadow like a carpet. Wild flowers bloom in the meadow. When you sit on a mat, the whole area is plum, camellia, and wild flowers. By the morning of the weekday, you can take a full charge of the flower world. It is a natural flower garden with no artificial additions.

Bohae Plum Farm, opened in 1978, is open to the public for free. The roof of the management office is open to the observation deck. The Plum Festival was held on March 16th and 17th this year, and the festival is noisy every year. Nanta performances, Pyongyang performing arts performances, Pungmul plaque performances, etc. are on the stage, and experience events such as plum face painting, kimchi making, and spring greens are included. Plum wine dipped with plums harvested directly from the plum farm is also sold.

A relaxing break in the flower garden
The plums harvested at the farms have different names depending on the processing method. Blue-green blue plum has a strong sour taste, and yellow ripe yellow plum has a good aroma. The smoked Omae peeled with Cheongmae is used as a medicinal herb, and pickled Cheongmae is dried in brine and is called Baekmae. Regardless of the name, plums are known to have excellent effects in helping fatigue and intestinal function. Besides plum wine, plum pickles, plum miso, etc. are definitely popular items near plum farms.
Plum bonsai
To enjoy the plum farm more quietly, it is better to use public transportation. First of all, if you don't have enough parking space and don't like the sad scene of flowers getting tangled in the car, use the bus even more. There are eight free buses from Mokpo Terminal to Haenam-eup via Sanmyeon. The bus stops at the entrance of Plum Farm. There is Songcheon Bus Stop right next door, but when you get off at Songcheon, you must walk. At Mokpo Terminal, you must take a slow ride to Sanmyeon, not direct to Haenam. It takes about 50 minutes to Bohae Farm. From Haenam General Terminal, a bus bound for Sanmyeon and Mokpo takes about 20 minutes to Bohae Farm. Given that the last train from Haenam to Seoul is cut at 17:30, it is good to use Haenam-Bohae Farm-Mokpo section properly considering the time required for the bus. Unfortunately, there is no information on bones and flesh that are accessed through public transportation on the county offices, plum farms, and festival websites. It is hard to find even on blogs. It takes about 20 minutes to walk from the entrance to the flower garden with the signboard of the Bohae Farm, which is a good way.
