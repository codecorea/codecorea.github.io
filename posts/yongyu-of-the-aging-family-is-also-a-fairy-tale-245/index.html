Eulwangni Beach is the western edge of Yeongjongdo. It goes up north along the beach, followed by Seonnyeo Baeui Beach, Eulwangni Beach, and Wangsan Beach. If you go further south, it is Muuido Island. In the summer of last year, SBS entertainment program <Running Man> was filmed here. Muuido Island connects Silmido Island to the north and Somuuido Island to the south. Somuuido has a small “small”, but it cannot be confined to its meaning. It has been the center of Muuido for a long time, and people have lived since 300 years ago. Muuido, a large island, was a ranch until the end of Joseon. A bridge was laid in 2011 between Somuuido Island and Muuido Island, and now it is a walking distance.
The road of Muui sea that goes around Somuuido is also spotlighted. The length is about 2.5km, and it is enough for an hour to walk by turtle. You have the pleasure of walking slowly through the island where you can't even hear the sound of a car engine. It is also romantic to look at the sea scenery under your feet from the pavilion of Ansan, the summit of the island. You can also see airplanes flying from and to Incheon International Airport. It is not too far from the land. I haven't heard a lot of word-of-mouth yet, so I enjoy a leisurely island trip.
On the other hand, Silmido in the north of Muuido Island is as famous as the movie title. Silmi Beach is a perfect place for the summer. Muido Island forms a double wall with Hana Beach on the main island. In Mui-do, Horyonggoksan is also a famous spot. You can embrace a wide view by light hiking. There are three islands, Modo, Shindo, and Shido, on the north side of Mui Island with Yeongjongdo in between. It leads to the bridge, aka Samhyungseom Island. There are drama shooting sets such as <Sad Love Story> and <Full House>. Baemikkumi Sculpture Park is very interesting in Modo, the very first island. Strange sculptures color the edge of the beach.
