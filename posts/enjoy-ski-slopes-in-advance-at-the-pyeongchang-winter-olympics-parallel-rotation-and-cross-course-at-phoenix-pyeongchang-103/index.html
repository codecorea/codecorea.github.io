Winter Olympic Games in Phoenix Pyeongchang
The Pyeongchang Winter Olympics Games, which will open on February 9, 2018, will take place in three cities, Pyeongchang, Gangneung and Jeongseon, Gangwon-do.
Skiing and boarding 9 events in Phoenix Pyeongchang
Three ski resorts, Phoenix Pyeongchang, Yongpyeong Resort, and Alpensia Resort, are located in Pyeongchang, where snow sports are held. This means that you can try out slopes for athletes before the Olympics. It's a thrilling news for those who ski and board a bit.
Skiing and boarding 9 events in Phoenix Pyeongchang
Phoenix Pyeongchang will host 5 freestyle skis and 4 snowboards during the Pyeongchang Winter Olympics snow games. Two athletes start at the same time to compete in parallel parallel rotation, a slope-style called the circus on snow, a moggle that uses techniques to turn and turn using jumps, the most exciting aerial among freestyle skiing, and air smoke on a semi-cylinder slope. Unfolding half-pipes and crosses that have to compete to speed through various obstacles. Six of the 21-side slopes of Phoenix Pyeongchang are used as Olympic venues.
There are eight lifts, six sunkids and eight-seater gondola.

The cross course that is open to the public along with the parallel run is exciting. Crosses take place on a variety of terrains, including banks, rulers, spines, and jumps.

Test event presented by top players
If you've experienced the slopes your players want to use, don't miss the test event at Phoenix Pyeongchang. Starting February 10, a test event will be held to prepare for the PyeongChang Winter Olympics. The test event is an international competition to check the preparations before the Olympics. It's a great chance to see some of the best players in the world before the Winter Olympics. FIS Freestyle Ski World Cup (Airreal, Moggle, Halfpipe) from February 10-19, FIS Snowboard World Cup (parallel, half-pipe) from February 12-19 do. Lee Sang-ho, who emerged as the expectation of the PyeongChang Olympic Games, and Sean White, the emperor of snowboarding, will participate in this snowboarding world cup.
