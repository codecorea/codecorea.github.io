Kang Hee-min was in office in Sejong 29 (1447), paying at the door and star hour. Among those who passed along with him at the time was Seongsammun (emergency paid to literature). He was well known as a prominent connoisseur, including participating in compilations such as GyeongGuk Daejeon, Dongmunseon, Dongguk Yeoji Seungram, and the rites of National Jockey, but he was also an agronomist who wrote a farm book such as Geumyang Miscellaneous. If you have visited Gwanokji Temple and Lotus Theme Park, you should see Incheon Sorae Wetland Ecological Park, about 8 ~ 9km away. You will feel the vitality and romance of the tidal flats while walking along the windy wetlands. Sorae Wetland Ecological Park is about 1.5 km from Sunaepo Soraepo Station.

Sorae Tidal Flat
Sorae Wetland Ecological Park was originally a Sorae Salt Farm. Sorae Salt Farm was produced in the mid 1930s and began producing salt. This salt was transported to Soonpo-gu by Incheon Expressway by narrow-line train or ship. These salts were then used as munitions for gunpowder. Sorae Salt Farm was closed in 1996 and was reborn as Sorae Wetland Ecological Park in 2009.
