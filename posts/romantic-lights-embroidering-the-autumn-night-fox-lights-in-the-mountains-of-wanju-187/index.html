Romantic lights embroidering the autumn night, fox lights in the mountains of Wanju

Wanju-gun, Jeollabuk-do, Korea

There is a light festival with the theme of the novel The Little Prince. The entire Wanju Hiljo Town nestled in Bongsil Mountain in Bibong-myeon, Wanju-gun, Jeollabuk-do turns into a romantic light festival in the evening. During the day, you can walk through the herb garden and fresh forests, and experience activities such as dirt play for children and Indian tents. Shall we go for a night walk while listening to the story of the fox?
There is no fox in the mountains, no fox.
The fox color appeared in Wanju. Old people said that when they encounter strange lights in a field or in a forest, they say it is a goblin or fox fire.
Come play fox in the mountains
The modern version of the goblin with LED bulbs on trees or sculptures can be enjoyed regardless of the weather because there is no risk of electric shock even in the event of snow or rain.
LED embroidered lights
Thanks to the fox in the novel 《The Little Prince》, the name ‘San Fox in the Mountains’ was added to the light festival that colored the night. It is a setting in which the fox who meets again in the dream of the little prince tells a story about him.
The story of “The Little Prince” is installed all over the place.
That doesn't mean that there are fox or young prince sculptures. The storyboards are installed everywhere with pictures. If you don't pay attention to the story board, you don't have to install any lights. There are also impressive passages from the novel, and advice to cherish this time. There is no fox, but you can enjoy the beautiful fox color by recalling the scene in the novel.
Purple gujeolcho blooming in autumn
Romantic indian tent
The festival hall can be considered as a large garden on a sloping mountain. There is a parking lot at the bottom, and when you climb the stairs, you will see a fox shelter selling ticket counters and snack streets.
Moonlight garden full of flowers and herbs
After passing the entrance with the welcome sign next to the ticket office, the road leads to the letter S, and the Moonlight Garden, Wind Garden, Media Art (Performance Hall), Rose Garden of the Little Prince, Mark Brown Cafe, Foxlight Glamping Site, Stone Tower Square, and Jabau Hwadukgui Restaurant Comes out. It is convenient to go to the top and then slowly descend and watch.
The moonlight garden is lit.
If you visit the fox school next to the ticket office during the day, you can experience various things. The ‘no-litter in and out’ using pottery pottery soil is a space where you can freely crush the earthen dough.
The rose that just lit up
The entrance time is set at 11, 2, and 4, so you can enjoy the experience only by going in time. You must bring your own clothes. If you make a reservation, you can also experience making a craftsman, a mensa game, and making a natural product.
month
The Fox Glamping Site, named Apios Village, has an Indian tent in the form of a cow hat. It is said that the Indian tent in the movie <Dancing with the Wolf> was reproduced and made by hand. It can sleep 4 to 6 people. It cannot be cooked inside, and breakfast and dinner are sold in a package provided by the restaurant.
Afios Village with Indian tent
When the lights come on, the romantic atmosphere is full. Sitting on a chair on the deck and looking down, the lights illuminate the mountain. It is a pity that the smell of the inside does not come off easily due to the structure of the tent.
Sit on a camping chair and enjoy the view
The rose garden where the little prince will fall in love
Beneath the camping grounds is Cafe Brown, where you can relax while drinking coffee and tea. Performances are held every Saturday evening on the stage underneath. The contents of the performance are different every time. Mostly, there are stages for amateur singers and bands active in the region as music performances. Sometimes the surprise proposals of lovers are held before or after the performance. If you are a couple considering a special event, please take courage to apply.
Girls in the rose garden
If you follow the rosy side of the theater, you will see the rose garden. It is a fantastic place with tens of thousands of roses in full bloom. Compared to other light festivals, the overall lighting decoration is rather modest, but thanks to the rose garden here, it is more impressive. Roses are not flowers, but harmony. A wire was connected to the stem to make the buds light up. At first glance, there are quite a few who are trying to break it because it is like a flower. It looks more plausible because it has two buds and a blooming rose. Roses that glow white, yellow, or pink spread out like a carpet and are beautiful.
A rose like a carpet
It looks pretty, but it is difficult to capture the feeling with a cell phone camera. To capture the romantic atmosphere, it is important not to use the flash, but to shoot as steady as possible.
A wind garden with a nice pine tree decoration
Beneath the rose garden is a moonlight garden full of herbs and flowers and a wind garden with colorful lights wrapped around trees. Moonlight Garden is better to see when there is sunlight before it gets dark. Flowers bloom every season, and various kinds of herbs are planted to make them fragrant. Fox lights in the mountains are open all year round, and the summer season (May-October) lights up at 6 PM and can be seen until 12 PM (last admission at 11:00). During the winter season (November-April), lighting is faster by 1 hour, and is available from 5 PM to 12 PM (last admission is 11 PM).

A bowl of noodles on the way to the light festival
Even if time is early on the way to the fox in the mountains, it is good to eat dinner beforehand. This is because there is only one restaurant in the festival hall and only a few snacks. It is a 15-minute drive from Bongdong-eup to Hiljo Town, so it is perfect to eat at Bongdong. Located on the other side of the Bongdong Nonghyup, “The Three Grandmothers Noodle Collection” is a place that has delighted people with delicious noodles for over 60 years.
Foxbit Shelter's Main Beak
There is only one menu, noodles. This is done by adding noodles to the broth, which has a deep rich taste and a refreshing taste, and sprinkle with red pepper powder on the onion. There is no common Zidane or Cucumber, but the chopsticks are delicious enough to be busy. Quickly empty a bowl and make the soup clean even though it is full of pears. It is very simple to put on top of noodles such as kimchi, kkakdugi, cheonyang pepper, and sweet radish. It is divided into large / medium / small depending on the quantity, and the price is also low at 5,000 won / 4,000 won / 3,500 won each. If you order ‘Medium’ for an adult male or ‘Small’ for a female, it is a quantity that can be left slightly due to the stomach. It's not splendid, but it's not special, but it has many long-standing regulars and a lot of people who come and eat it on purpose.
