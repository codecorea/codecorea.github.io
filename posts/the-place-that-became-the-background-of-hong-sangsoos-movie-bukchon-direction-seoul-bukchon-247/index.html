A walk around Bukchon where the past and present are evenly mixed
The places that set the background for the film slowly fill the corners of Bukchon. As you walk, Bukchon's specialty comes out naturally. These days, the Bukchon walk is conducted with various themes. There are also good places to take a commemorative photo called 'Bukchon Eight Views'.
Gye-dong alley filled with residents' lives
The hanok alley in the 31st district of Gahoe-dong is the best preserved hanok in Bukchon. The Gye-dong alley from the modern office building to the Jungang High School contains the lives of old residents, such as old baths, clinics, and barber shops. In addition to this, Bukchon's main walking course is the Gahoe-dong workshop alley with open hanoks and workshops, the Sagan-dong gallery alley with galleries, and the school alley in front of Deokseong Girls' High School, where many restaurants are located. If you take a simple route called 'Bukchon Walk' before going on a walk in the alley, it will serve as a good guide.
