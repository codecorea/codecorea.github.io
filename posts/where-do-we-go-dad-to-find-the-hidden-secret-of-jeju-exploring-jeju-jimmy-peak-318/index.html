Scenery where the sea and sky meet

Jimmy Bong that stands on the shore of Hadori Village in Gujwa-eup, Jeju. Jimmy Peak, also known as Jeju Island's end of the earth, is a superb view of Udo, Seongsan Ilchulbong, Migratory Birds, and Mt. Halla as well as the emerald sea. It is one of the most representative representatives of the eastern Jeju region. It was one of Jeju's hidden sights that only those who knew so far knew, but as it was included in the Olle 21 course, which opened last year, many people climbed the peaks along the Olle trail.
