Insadong & Jongno next to Jogyesa Temple… From temple food shops to simple vegetarian shops
There are a lot of specialty restaurants around Jongno & Insa-dong, where Jogyesa Temple is located. Temple food, commonly referred to as “zebap,” is literally their food. According to the Buddhist doctrine that prohibits 'killing', it has a natural vegetarian diet and tastes without coming (green onions, garlic, soothe, leek, red cherries). The taste of modern man, who is domesticated by various seasonings and seasonings, is boring to the mouth, but after eating, many people find it comfortable and healthy.
