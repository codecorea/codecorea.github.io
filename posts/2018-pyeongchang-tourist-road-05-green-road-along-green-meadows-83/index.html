2018 Pyeongchang Tourist Road 05, Green Road along green meadows

Pyeongchang-gun, Gangwon, South Korea

<Travel Course>

Alpensia Ski Jumping Team →→ Daegwanryeong Sky Ranch → Anvandegi Unyu-gil →→ Yunyu Village

From Pyeongchang Daegwallyeong, Gangneung's journey is full of green. The skies that make you look at the ski observatory and the sky ranch where the sun shines as transparent as a child's smile are all green. Thick greens were formed on the cabbage leaves of the young bed bug, which barely made its way through the low clouds.
Alpensia Ski Jumping University, which was established for the 2018 Pyeongchang Winter Olympics, made its name public with the movie <National Representative>. The ridge with Alpensia ski jump is 850 meters above sea level. It is about 100m higher than the lower ground with the stands. You have to climb 69m to the observatory again, so the total height is close to 1,000m. Of course, only when the sea level as a base is dizzy.
Alpensia Ski Jumping Slope
Ski jumping stage viewing is divided into general viewing, which includes only the observation deck, and special viewing including the K98 ski jumping stage. The admission fee is 2,000 won for general visit and 6,000 won for special visit.
Alpensia Ski Jumping Monorail Upper Boarding Point
The K98 Ski Jumping Team has been open to the public since April 2015. The view is an art only in the 25m high K98 ski jump. Mount Balwang and Gorupogi spread out like a panorama. As well as the view, the passage between the waiting room and the start line is also a specialty. It is made of lattice reinforcement to prevent snow from accumulating, so it looks down 25m. It feels like you're walking on a skywalk covered with tempered glass. Be careful not to drop your phone or wallet.
Ski jumping practice scene of athletes
If you take the elevator to the observation deck, your view becomes wider. It's great to sit in the lounge cafe and enjoy a cup of coffee, or to enjoy the alpine law of Pyeongchang.
Ski jumping practice scene of athletes
The 11-seater monorail climbs from the sports park main stadium to the ski jump entrance. Groups can also use the car. The monorail runs every 30 minutes from 9:25 am. If you visit early in the morning, you can see the players practicing.
Alpensia Ski Jumping Observatory
Don't miss the Daegwallyeong skiing history building on the first floor of the main stadium. From the story of Uru, the god of skiing in Icelandic myths, to the modern age, this is a space where you can examine the development of skiing by age. Traditional skis made of wood and straw snow are also interesting.
Daegwallyeong Ski History Museum
Daegwallyeong Sky Ranch is a nature-compliant ecological ranch. By minimizing man-made facilities, nature and people, people and animals are designed to blend naturally. More than four times more than Yeouido, grassland is full of green. It's thrilling just to step into the transparent scenery resembling the summer sunshine.
Daegwallyeong Sky Ranch spread out like a carpet
Tractor carriages make it easier to get around the ranch. The huge wagon pulled by the tractor makes a round trip through the ranch through the Skytop Observatory. You can reach the observation deck in 15 minutes by car. From the observation deck, you can see the view of the ranch and Hoengye-eup. You can also walk slowly from the observation deck to the top of Seonryeongryeong. This 1.8km long road is called the Scenic Landscape. There are many beautiful trekking courses in the sky ranch, including 'edge forest trail', 'forest trails' and 'jongjong walking trail'. Tractor wagons run flexibly depending on the number of visitors.
Cow pasture
There is a lot of space in the sky ranch to hang out with the animals. Representatives include a baby zoo where baby animals such as calves, foals and lambs are gathered, and a sheep farm where you can experience hay cycle in the flock. You can experience hay cycle in both places. The sheep herd is operated organically depending on the condition of the sheep. Horseback riding is an indispensable experience of Sky Ranch. Horse riding experience is available for anyone over the age of 7 to ride a round track on horseback. Equestrian trekking on horseback walks through the forest trails of the sky ranch. Horse trekking is available on a advance reservation basis only for those who can ride the canter.
Daegwallyeong Sky Ranch filled with green light
Anbandong is a Gangneung dialect of Anbanduk. Anban refers to a wooden board that is used as a base for baking bread. It is named after the rugged Baekdudaegan stem, which is like a rice cake and is rich in terrain. Anbandongi, located at an altitude of 1100m, is the highest village inhabited by Koreans. 195 ha of farmland is spread in the shape of an eagle wing with Okneungbong and Mt. Gorupogi on the left and right. In 1995, farmers bought land that had been allowed to be reclaimed from state land and leased to Hwajeon people. If the rice paddy field shows an aspect of the hard life of the seashore people, the Anbandi highland field is a miracle created by the sweat and efforts of the Chinese people.
Anbandongi Sunrise
Early settlers cultivated rough stones in slopes with only pickaxes and shovels. The result of the stacked stone is the yoke observatory. It is a space filled with the spirit of pioneering and pioneering spirit of a group of Hwajeon people. If you turn to the right at the crossroads that are 300 meters above Unyuchon Parking Lot, you will see Yoke Observatory.
Andangigi Milk Road
The entire 6km Anbandigi Unyugil must walk slowly like clouds flowing through the wind. You don't have to walk fast. The path that begins at the bottom of the mountain builds up one by one, one after another, to the hillside and back to the summit. The higher the road, the wider the view. The cabbages that have not yet grown by the way are neat.
Anbandongi Unyugil Highland Cabbage Field
Standing in the heliport under Okneungbong, the blue water of the East Sea dimly behind the meandering cabbage field. From the heliport, the sunrise observatory leads to Unyuchon, the starting point, along the ridge flowing smoothly. The Anbandigi Unyu-gil is the origin return course, so you can return to the Yunyu village no matter which direction you take.
Andangigi Milk Road
Yunyu Village is the head of Anbandigi Unyu-gil. It is too small to call it a village. All three houses are next to the town hall. It is a space used as a restaurant and accommodation. The empty lot in front of the lodge and restaurant, called Unyu Store, is the only public parking lot in Unyu Village. Narrow bus lanes make it difficult for large buses, but the youngest car can be parked. The two lodges were named Unyu-woo and Unyu-taek respectively. If the road of cloud flows, the cloud and cloud will be the house where the cloud rests.
Yunyu Village
Both places have the appearance of you and the house where Hwajeonmin lived. The interior is as clean as any pension. There is a living room and bedroom and a bathroom with shower. Kitchen facilities are perfect. The fun thing is Agung, which is reproduced as it was. Although there is a boiler, it is a space where you can cook rice or cook potatoes or sweet potatoes in a cauldron placed on a fire.
Unyuchon in the shape of a house
The Hwajeon Minryo Museum, which is also a canteen, is located at a crossroads 300 meters above Unyuchon Parking Lot. You can take a look at the life of the Koreans in the 1970s and 1980s.
Unyuchon Hwajeon Folk Museum
