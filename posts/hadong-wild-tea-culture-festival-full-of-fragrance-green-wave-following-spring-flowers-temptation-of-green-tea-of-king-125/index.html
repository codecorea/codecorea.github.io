Hadong Wild Tea Culture Festival, full of fragrance, green wave following spring flowers, temptation of 'Green Tea of King'

Hadong-gun, Gyeongnam, Korea

From May 19th to 22th, the king's green tea! The 22nd Hadong Wild Tea Culture Festival will be held under the theme of Hadong, a place with a thousand years of incense and fashion. Before the festival began, we decided to catch the end of the spring and take a look at Hadong's wild tea gardens along the Seomjin River.
If there is green tea in Boseong, Jeonnam, there is wild tea in Hadong, Gyeongnam. Hadong wild tea is a specialized crop of which 2,000 farms in Hwagae and Akyang-myeon earn 18 billion won annually. The tea plantation area is over 1000ha (10 million㎡, about 3 million pyeong). Hwagae-myeon is close to Seomjin River, so it is foggy, humid, and has a great environment for growing tea trees at large crossroads. Is that so?
