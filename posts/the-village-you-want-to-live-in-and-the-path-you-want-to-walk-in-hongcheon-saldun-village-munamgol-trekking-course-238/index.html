Munamgol Trekking Course for walking in spring and autumn
Walk the mountain path colored with autumn leaves at the peak of autumn

Hongcheon is colored with autumn leaves before Seoul. Moreover, the altitude of high altitude is high, so Saldun grows vegetables more quickly. The Moonamgol trekking course in the third week of October is also in full bloom with reddish-yellow leaves. Munsan Island has a bright light. The life of a person walking while looking at the red and yellow leaves is also a way to mature.
It is the Munamgol Trekking Course when you cross one from Saldun Village. However, there is no bridge, so you have to go 3 to 4 km by car. From the main road, follow the signpost of the hollyhock and the road begins.

This course is called 'the way you want to walk' for those who like to walk. Walk 4 to 5 km one way, 8 to 10 km round trip, and walk back to the three-way intersection where Yuljeon 2-ri ends. If you keep walking, you will see Yuljeon 3-ri, and the road continues up the road. There is no reason to wander because it is an external road to Samgeori. If you go up to the three-way intersection with a small bridge and then come back, it is enough to walk for 2-3 hours.

Weekend in the dark

There are many ways to enjoy Saldun, but how about spending a day at the Sangdun Campus Camping Site, which is made using the closed school? It's also a good idea to give yourself a weekend filled with nature. The Saldun Valley flows beside the camping ground. There is no place like this as a place for playing in the summer. It is safe to wear a life jacket because the water is deep, but it is free to rent to campers at the campsite. Bicycles can be rented for free, and movies are shown outdoors on the weekends.

Compared to other campsites with clear site divisions, the attractiveness of Sangdun Branch Camping Camp using a closed school and a valley is natural. You can set up tents where you want around the playground and valley and enjoy the day buried in nature. The daily fee for a camping site is 25,000 won per weekday per tent, and 30,000 won per weekend and peak season.
Saengdun Branch Camping Site in the center of Saldun Village
Saengdun Branch Camping Camp, which can hold 30 tents, is jointly operated by Saldun Village. You can also stay in the pension if you are troubled with camping by operating 5 pensions right next door. Rental camping sites are available free of charge for travelers staying in pensions. The campground is only open until the third weekend in November and is closed in winter.
A natural camping ground where you can enjoy a long break

Instead, in winter, you can enjoy camping at Saldun Cottage. The hut is a mixture of Japanese-style houses and temples. There are 3 rooms on the 1st floor, and there is a living room on the 2nd floor where you can drink tea and view the scenery. Camping is also available in the yard. Camping is open all year round and costs 30,000 won on weekdays and 40,000 won on weekends per site.
