"This place in Wonju!" Industrial tourism connecting chocolate, nature, medical equipment, kitchenware, cosmetics

Wonju, Gangwon, South Korea

The village of Wonju, the gateway to Gangwon-do and the name of Gangwon-do. It is home to the island river and Ganhyeon Amusement Park, boasting clear waters, including Chiaksan (1288m), a large mountain in Yeongseo. Today, I will travel to find a different expression of Wonju. The name is a bit unfamiliar, but if you know it, the fun is 'Industrial Tourism'. Introducing experience-based tours and industrial tourism hotspots for families.
#One. Gather everyone who likes chocolate! Chocolate Empress
Chocolate Empress (CEO Jang Ji-eun), located in Haenggu-dong, Wonju-si, is a restaurant specializing in fermentation chocolate that adds special taste and efficacy to chocolate. Naturally fermented chocolate made after 12 years of investment is matured in Onggi for at least 3 days to give a deep taste.
The building of Chocolate Empress, called Sontak Hotel, is a space for making fermented chocolate and fermented beverages, as well as a cafe, laboratory, and experience space where you can taste them. If you go to this place, you can find a mysterious chocolate that can be stored for more than 100 days by fermentation aging without additives.
There is an experience program for both children and adults. In addition to making bear fermented chocolate for kids 4 to 6 years old (40 ~ 60 minutes, 18,000 won per person) and making cacao cake balls for more than kindergarten students (40 ~ 60 minutes, 15,000 won per person) , Making petals fermented chocolate that can be experienced by elementary school students (60 ~ 90 minutes, 25,000 won per person) and chocolate fondue (30 minutes, 10,000 won per person), cacao cake bar (about 30 minutes, 1 person 1 person) 15,000 won).
Raw chocolate can be stored for a long time by natural fermentation
Many people are also looking for a hand drip coffee experience (90 minutes, 25,000 won per person) for high school students and older. All programs can be applied for more than 4 people. Reservation required one day in advance.

#2. Healing of body and mind, <Wonju Herb Farm>
Wonju Herb Farm (CEO, Cha Seong-hwan), proud of Wonju, Gangwon-do. Nearly 1,000 species of plants are growing in various gardens and ponds, including rose gardens, livestock gardens, training gardens, indoor gardens, and herb bath gardens.
Main building where you can find herbal drinks, food and products
In particular, the night view and herb foot bath in the colorful garden with thousands of LED lights are popular as a date course of Wonju. The herb foot bath that cats welcome is added to the fee (1000 won).
Ponds filled with outdoor gardens
Wonju Herb Farm is largely divided into indoor buildings and outdoor spaces. The plant museum described above fills the outdoor space, while the three-story building houses cafes, restaurants, and herb shops. You can taste herbal teas, herbal ice cream, herbal pork cutlet and bibimbap, offering a variety of herbal experiences to those looking for Wonju Herb Farm.

#4. <Neoflam> full of colorful eco-friendly kitchen utensils
Neo-Flam (CEO Park Chang-soo), a company specializing in eco-friendly kitchen products, loved by housewives, newlyweds, and self-employed students, built a new nest in Wonju Enterprise City. Neo-Flam, famous for its colorful kitchen utensils, is a combination of 'neo' and 'flam'. Eco-friendly ceramic coated kitchen utensils are attracting the attention of modern people.
Unfortunately, it is difficult to personally visit the Neoflam factory. So far, only groups of 40 or more are available for field trips. You can see a series of products produced in the open factory with transparent windows, and you can also watch and purchase them in the showroom, where various products of Neoflam are displayed. As it is purchased in the field, it can be purchased at a lower price than commercially available. The showroom, which offers pretty kitchen utensils, is impressive.
