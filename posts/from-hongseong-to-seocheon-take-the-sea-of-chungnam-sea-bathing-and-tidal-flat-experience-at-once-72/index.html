Seocheon-gun, Chungnam, Korea

What is the west coast's greatest attraction? The West Sea is filled with charm that is different from the East Sea, which attracts street hands with clear and beautiful water. You can enjoy sea bathing on the boardwalk and enjoy the tidal-flat experience under the influence of high and low tide. So prepared. Along the coast of Chungnam, head to the gem-like beach and tidal-flat experience hotspots located in Hongseong, Boryeong, and Seocheon!
Before starting the mudflat experience, let's know this!
In order to enjoy the tidal-flat experience, some preparations are required. If you have comfortable shoes such as boots or sneakers, or a basket or a basket for fish and shellfish, you do not need to rent them. Some of the rental fees are included in the experience fee and some are separate. At low tide there are many beaches where you can enjoy clamming for free. Extra clothes are also required.
The tidal-flat of Cheonsu Bay is caught with various foods, of which clams are the most common. Going south, the clams, the clams and the lilies are added. Familiar clams can be caught at all times except during the spawning season, from early July to mid-August. Don't worry about overlapping with summer vacation. Most experience centers manage and divide the experience zone, so visitors can collect their clams.
