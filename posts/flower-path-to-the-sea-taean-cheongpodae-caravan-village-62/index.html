Camping is a taste of equipment, but I don't want to do that. . Then the alternative is caravans! Camping atmosphere to the fullest, more comfortable than my home base, there is a great view of the West Sea and upscale trails! Cheongpodae Caravan Village is such a place.

Cheongpodae Caravan Village overlooks the vast West Sea in the future and leans back to the pine forest. The campsite, run by a young broken woman, has eight caravans. They are all from the waters of the caravan's original country of `` Mikuk ''.

Cheongpodae Caravan Village's most prominent feature is its 'large'. A total of eight caravans are big at a glance. "Others are for 4-6 people, but we operate for couples." The owner's explanation. Smart! Try to get inside. 'Isn't it a caravan?' The prominent courtesy misses nicely. In addition to the double-bed queen size bedroom, the kitchen, living room and bathroom have a shower booth. Flower path to Cheongpodae Beach
Go up 300m to the right from the entrance of Cheongpodae Caravan Village and you will find many nice pensions with views of the sea. In front of the pension, there is a beautiful flower garden on the white sand beach.
Flower path to Cheongpodae Beach
The flower paths that seem to come out of fairy tales unfold in the background of the sea. Especially, yellow wildflowers forming a colony attract visitors.
Cheongpodae Beach Boardwalk
The flower path of seduction leads to the sea, and the seaside road parallel to the West Sea provides a nice walk for Cheongpodae visitors.
