[Korea Travel Bucket List Part 3] This summer's Jeju Island bike tour!

Jeju, Korea

Sejong (pseudonym) in his late 40s with the motto of "The journey continues until life is over." He set the summer vacation as Jeju Island. Jeju Island is not the first. Nevertheless, I chose Jeju Island without hesitation because of the expectation of a new trip. What caught Sejong's heart is the coastal round road called 'Jeju Fantastic Bike Path'.
The Jeju Fantastic Bike Path is a course that cycles around Jeju Island along a coastal road with little traffic. It was opened in November 2015. Sejong says the reason for choosing a bicycle tour.
"In the light of my previous experience, I thought I could make the trip enjoyable. When I walked Jeju Olle, I saw a lot of young friends traveling on bicycles, so I also wanted to travel like this one day, and after the trip, I felt like it. "
Even though he grew older and became a middle-aged body, his mind was always young, so Sejong decided to take a bicycle trip to Jeju Island.
Bicycle travel is a new challenge to travel to Jeju Island
Bicycle travel counterclockwise
Sejong's challenge course is 21km from Yongduam Certification Center to Darak Shelter Certification Center. This is the first course of the Jeju Fantastic Bicycle Path. There is a reason for his choice. It would be nice to implement a grand plan called "Jongju" at once, but it is very unlikely. I know that like the mind, the body won't be able to keep up, so I know I have to try it many times. In that case, I decided that it would be better to try in order from the first course. He plans to turn Jeju Island counterclockwise along the coast. That way, you can run facing the sea.

Sejong has two colleagues to accompany. Put your body on the bike. I never think it will be difficult. It seemed easier than walking. 'Olle also walked, it's about a bike', 'If anyone knows how to ride a bicycle, anyone can do it. I think it's never hard, 'and press the pedal vigorously. Bike trip is a trip to enjoy nature
<Jeju Fantastic Bicycle Path>
Ten courses have been developed around the certification center. The length is 240 km. If you run 70 ~ 80km a day, you can do it if you have 2 nights 3 days or 3 nights 4 days. Even running 30km takes 7-8 days. It doesn't matter if you don't have the skills, but don't be greedy. It is important to move on a fixed schedule even if you go slowly. The accommodation is selected in advance according to the route.
<Bicycle Tips>
-Basic safety equipment is essential. Helmets, gloves, knee and elbow guards, etc., should be provided for safe riding.
-Prepare long-sleeved clothes, goggles, sunblocks, etc. in preparation for heat, radiant heat, and sunlight exposure.
-Prepare a repair kit and spare tube that can be repaired in the event of a simple malfunction such as a tire puncture. If you can't do it yourself, check the repair shop contact information.
-Headlights and tail lights are essential when driving at night
-Be familiar with the left and right brake directions and how to operate the transmission.
-The saddle is moderately high enough to reach the ground.
-Since it is necessary to ride a bicycle for a long time, it is also helpful to prepare functional clothes.
