Naksansa Temple, Yangyang - Yangyang-gun, Gangwon-do, Korea

Gangwon-do, where freshness goes and the clouds rest. At the end of last winter, too many snow clouds were resting. The ever-falling snow became a natural disaster, causing great damage to Gangwon-do. In 2005, a wildfire that came over in a strong wind hit Naksansa Temple. Ironically, it was Arbor Day on April 5. The forest fires caused Naksansa to be close to the battlefield. I couldn't help but be sorry for the sight of the burning of the cylinder and the melting of the same Joseon dynasty designated as a treasure.
