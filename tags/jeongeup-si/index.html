Manchurian Road Trip to Jeongeup Ssanghwacha Street - Jeongeup-si, Jeollabuk-do, Korea

In the season when the warmth is missed, go to the street of Ssanghwacha in Jeongeup, where you can smell Gujeolcho. The heartache floating in my head dries up in a cup of hot Ssanghwa-tang, and the weight of everyday life when I press my shoulders becomes loose. If you eat the sweet chestnuts and jujube contained in the dark Ssanghwatang, you will feel as if you were in vain. If a warm cup of hot water delivered to a goblet teacup and a cup of sweet Ssanghwa-tang warms the body gently, it can withstand the chilly weather and the harsh world.
