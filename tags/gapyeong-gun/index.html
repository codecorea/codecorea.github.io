A feast of beautiful light to enjoy in winter, Gapyeong Morning Calm Arboretum - Gapyeong-gun, South Korea

On the way to the Moonlight Garden past the hometown garden, a long tunnel that looks like hanging pink petals awaits. As if you are just starting to love, in the pink tunnel, pink smile flowers bloom on the faces of lovers. The entrance from the morning square to the Moonlight Garden is like a fairy tale country. Giant bears and mushrooms, all sorts of animals and a sled-pulling Rudolph sit amongst the larch. The white church with an angel inside is the most popular photo zone. Gapyeong-gun, South Korea. Winter fairy tale meets in the snowy arboretum, Morning Calm Arboretum - Gapyeong-gun, Gyeonggi-do, Korea

When it was snowy winter, I visited the morning calm arboretum, a winter kingdom that glistens with a brilliant autumnal glow. Many people visit the arboretum even in this season thanks to the “Five Starlight Garden” held every winter. When the darkness fell, I followed the colored lights blooming. It is also suitable for a quiet downtown dating course. A winter festival of lights held at this time of year. The five-pointed starlight garden, which celebrated its eighth anniversary this year, will continue until March 15, when the vegetation is asleep. Gapyeong-gun, Gyeonggi-do, Korea. Korea Tour
