Are you curious about Jeju? If you are curious! Jeju pony experience park - Seogwipo, Jeju, Korea

Located in the middle mountainous area of Pyoseon-myeon, Jeju, Kashiri Village is a historic town where 600-year-old pastoral culture lives and breathes. It is the site of the Noksanjang, the largest of all the mountain horses in Jeju, where the old horses were raised, and Gapjangjang, the breeding site for the best horses of the Joseon Dynasty. There is a pony experience park that contains the history and culture of the old town, and it has long been a place where it has been considered as the best horse pasture since ancient times. The pony experience park is equipped with accommodation facilities such as a pony museum and horseback riding, as well as a guest house and a camping ground, making it a perfect destination for two days and one night. Tour of Geojedo Gujo La Village. Geojedo, the head of a raised head - Geoje-si, Gyeongnam, Korea

Geojedo National Highway 14 is famous for its beautiful scenery. After passing Buksan Mountain and heading south to the sea, an impressive village appears. It is a gujo-la village that crosses the sea next to a land that looks as long as a giraffe's neck and thin like an ant's waist. There is a small mountain beyond the narrow and long town, so the impression that you want to look around is imminent. Entered the Gujo La Village as attracted by the strange charm. It's past winter, but the weather seems a bit winter, and it's too cold for spring, so it's the south side of the peninsula that shines. Jeju Island is fine, but Geoje Island is a good alternative when transportation is burdensome. It leads to Geoje Bridge, so it is easy to access and has a warm atmosphere. Beyond Geoje Bridge, you can enjoy the scenery of Namhae next to it. There are peaceful seas, islands with grains of grain, roofs gathered on the shores of fishing villages, and ships anchored in the harbor. Among them, the unique features of Geoje Island are the complex Rias coastline and the surrounding landscape. It has become monotonous with people's hands, but the coastline, which is infrequent in nature, can be seen with its intricate coastline. Geojedo Gujo La Villageのツアー。 コジェド、上げられた頭の頭 - 韓国慶南市巨済市

巨済島国道14号は美しい景色で有名です。 北山を通過して南に海に向かうと、印象的な村が現れます。 キリンの首と同じくらい長く、アリの腰のように細く見える土地の隣の海を渡るグジョラ村です。 狭くて長い町の向こうに小さな山があるので、見回したいという印象が差し迫っています。 不思議な魅力に惹かれて郡上ラ村に入った。 過去の冬ですが、天気は少し冬のようで、春には寒すぎるので、輝くのは半島の南側です。 済州島は大丈夫ですが、交通が面倒な場合は巨済島が良い選択肢です。 巨済橋に通じているので、アクセスしやすく、温かい雰囲気です。 巨済橋を越えて、隣の南海の景色を楽しむことができます。 穏やかな海、穀物の入った島、漁村の海岸に集まった屋根、港に停泊している船があります。 中でも巨済島のユニークな特徴は、リアスの複雑な海岸線と周囲の風景です。 人の手で単調になりましたが、自然界ではめったにない海岸線は、その複雑な海岸線で見ることができます。 巨済島の東側の海岸線に沿って巨済大路に乗り、郡上村まで南下します。 韓国慶南市巨済市.
